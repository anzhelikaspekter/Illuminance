Our Strategy

We build our strategy around three key principles:

  • Capital Preservation

    The primary goal is to protect the initial investment. Funds are allocated into a pool with strict risk limits, minimizing potential losses even during periods of high market volatility.

  • Financial Cushion Formation

    A portion of profits is set aside in a reserve to provide additional capital protection and prepare for an increase in position volumes.

  • Margin Expansion and Compound Interest

    Using the financial cushion, we expand positions and reinvest profits, creating a compound interest effect. This approach allows for exponential capital growth and increasing profitability over time.

Advantages of the Illuminance Approach

  • Systematic capital protection.
  • Diversification and risk control.
  • Capital growth through compound interest and increasing trading volumes.
