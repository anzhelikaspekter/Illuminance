Components of the Illuminance ecosystem

Artificial intelligence, marketing, news, and tokenization work together to create stable growth. Every user becomes part of a network that rewards activity and fosters community. This is a platform that transforms innovations into real profit opportunities.

  • Trading robots and artificial intelligence: Innovative trading algorithms developed for Illuminance Node and based on quantum technologies provide a stable passive income. Every action within the platform is rewarded with token points, enhancing profits and encouraging participation.

  • Marketing Agency: The in-house agency Illuminance assists partners in promoting their personal and corporate brands. Referral programmes, bonuses, promotions, and giveaways create a dynamic ecosystem that is beneficial for all participants.

  • Built-in messenger: Communication is key to growth. We are creating an integrated messenger where users can chat, create team chats, VIP groups, and channels featuring the founders of Illuminance. Each level of entrepreneurs gains access to new communication formats.

  • News Portal: Stay at the heart of events. We are developing our own news hub that covers current topics in the world of technology, investments, artificial intelligence, and financial markets.

  • Token and tokenisation: The internal token Illuminance will be the core of the ecosystem: it will facilitate payments for services, provide benefits, enable participation in projects, and enhance income. This is an internal economy that grows alongside each user.

  • Digital asset storage: Security and convenience are our priorities. Partners will be able to earn, store, and manage crypto assets in a secure environment, choosing whether to withdraw funds or use the internal Illuminance storage.
