Illuminance Philanthropic Initiatives

Responsibility as Part of Strategy

At Illuminance, we believe that true progress is impossible without social responsibility.

Financial success and technological advancement must go hand in hand with supporting the people for whom these technologies open the path to a better future.

That is why philanthropy for us is not an addition, but an integral part of our sustainable growth strategy. Every Illuminance initiative is aimed at making the world smarter, kinder, and fairer.

Digital Future — Education and Technology for Tomorrow

We invest in children, because they are the ones who shape the world of tomorrow.

The Digital Future program provides schools in developing countries with modern equipment and digital learning tools.

Access to technology opens new opportunities for children — to study, grow, and create their future on equal terms.

Children of Light — Supporting Talent

We believe that every talent is a source of light.

The Children of Light program offers grants to schoolchildren and students who demonstrate skills in science, mathematics, and technology.

These grants help children from underprivileged families continue their education, participate in international competitions, and bring their own projects to life.

Investing in talent means investing in the progress of society as a whole.

Illuminance Humanity — Aid in Times of Crisis

The world is vulnerable to unpredictable disasters and crises.

Illuminance Humanity provides targeted support to families affected by natural disasters and humanitarian catastrophes: temporary housing, essential supplies, and financial assistance for recovery.

We believe that protection is the first step toward growth — which is why we safeguard not only capital but also human lives.
