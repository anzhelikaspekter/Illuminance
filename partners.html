Digital Bank — A Deposit Fueled by Action

Partners gain access to a unique tool — the Digital Bank.

How It Works

  • The company allocates margin funds for the deposit, granting partners access to capital without the need for personal investment.

  • Profit is earned only through the active attraction of new partner investments, which gradually replace the margin funds with real assets.

  • This mechanism ensures that returns grow proportionally to the partner’s activity, combining the use of leveraged capital with the expansion of their investment network.

Benefits for Partners

This way, a partner’s income is generated by actions — promotion, education, network growth — not by direct investments.

It makes participation in the Illuminance ecosystem accessible, safe, and profitable at the same time.

  • Capital Safety: personal funds are not exposed in the margin deposit.

  • Scalable Income: returns increase alongside network growth and incoming investor capital.

  • Transparent Mechanism: profit accrual is directly linked to real activity and the attraction of investments.

  • Efficient Use of Resources: partners gain access to company capital to increase returns without risking their own assets.
